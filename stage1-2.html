Stage I: L Layer

2. Last Two Corners of L Layer

There are many possible configurations in this step. This section shows selected solutions for all possible situations (at least 3 turns long). Sometimes the initial U-turn (U, U' or U2) may be necessary to obtain one of the following situations. As you can see, all sequences are at most 6-turns long (the 6-turn sequences do not need an initial U-turn). This sets maximum number of face turns to solve the first four corners to 9 (3 + 6). However, the average number of moves should not exceed 8 turns.
